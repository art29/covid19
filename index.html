ORDER FACE SHIELDS

If you need PPE face shields for your hospital or for other medical insitutions, Contact Us through the form below !

Our team work with medical professionals to improve the face shields every day ! We will try to do our best to get them to you as fast as possible, but please note that it might take a few days to process and print the face shields.



HOW CAN YOU HELP ?

First, the easiest thing that you can do to help with the COVID-19 pandemic is to stay home unless you absolutely need to. If you need to please follow the social distancing policies (2 meters or 6 feet from everyone).

Another way you can help out if you have a 3D Printer is to print some parts for the Face Shields. The file is accessible here.

Steps for 3D Printing

  • Download the STL File and make sure you can print it (Dimension, Good quality ...)
  • Print them but please make sure you use gloves when touching the parts so they are NOT contaminated.
  • Use this form form to let us know that you printed some
  • Drop them to the makerspace or get them picked up
  • We will then assemble them and send them to different medical facilities in the region !
  • Thanks for your help ! If you want to do more, go ahead, we will be more than happy to get some help !
